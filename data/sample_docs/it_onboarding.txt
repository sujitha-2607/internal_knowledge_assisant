IT Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the company! This document outlines your basic IT setup.

1. Email:
   - Your email ID will be first.last@company.com
   - Login through Outlook or Webmail using SSO credentials.

2. VPN Access:
   - Required for remote work and server access.
   - Download FortiClient from internal tools portal.

3. Software:
   - Teams, Slack, Jira, Zoom pre-installed on your laptop.
   - Additional software can be requested through the IT Helpdesk.

4. Passwords:
   - Change all temporary passwords within 24 hours.
   - Use strong passwords with at least 12 characters.

Need help? Submit a ticket at helpdesk.company.com or email it-support@company.com.
