Company Leave Policy 2024

1. Annual Leave:
   - Every full-time employee is entitled to 20 paid annual leave days per year.
   - Leave accrues monthly.

2. Sick Leave:
   - Employees get 10 paid sick days per year.
   - A medical certificate is required if absent for more than 2 days.

3. Maternity/Paternity Leave:
   - Maternity leave: 26 weeks (paid)
   - Paternity leave: 2 weeks (paid)

4. Unpaid Leave:
   - Can be requested with manager approval.
   - Subject to HR review and availability.

To apply for leave, use the company HR portal. Ensure leave requests are submitted at least 7 days in advance (except emergencies).
